Tens of thousands of developers earn money by developing apps in the APP Store and Google Play, so of course you can earn money in the Blocklet Store too.

By setting the payment field in the `blocklet.yml` file, you can easily set the currency and amount of payments, and you can even **customize the percentage of payments to the Blocklet Store!**
